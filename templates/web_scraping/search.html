Search Tips
Effective Searching
  • Use quotes for exact phrases - "climate change solutions"
  • Be specific - Rather than "vaccines", try "COVID-19 vaccine effectiveness studies"
  • Include alternative terms - "election integrity OR election security"
  • Scope your search - "misinformation Facebook 2022" is better than just "misinformation"
Search Engine Functionality

The search function uses popular search engines to find content matching your query. The system then extracts and analyzes the content from the results.
