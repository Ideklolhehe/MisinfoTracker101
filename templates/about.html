About CIVILIAN

Countering Information Vilification with Intelligence, Logic, Intelligence, Analysis, and Networks

Our Mission

CIVILIAN is an advanced machine intelligence system designed to detect, trace, and autonomously dismantle global misinformation through real-time multilingual narrative surveillance and counter-intervention capabilities.

Key Components

  • Multi-Source Data Ingestion: Collects and analyzes data from diverse sources including social media, news outlets, and specialized feeds.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Continuously monitors for emerging misinformation narratives across multiple platforms.
  • Advanced Analysis: Uses AI to analyze narrative patterns, propagation methods, and source credibility.
  • Counter Messaging: Generates evidence-based counter-narratives to combat detected misinformation.
  • Belief Graph Analysis: Maps connections between narratives, sources, and propagation networks.

Technology Stack

CIVILIAN utilizes a sophisticated technology stack including:

  • Natural Language Processing for content analysis
  • Machine Learning for pattern detection and anomaly identification
  • Graph Database for representing complex narrative relationships
  • Vector Search for semantic similarity matching
  • Multi-agent system architecture for specialized tasks

Data Ethics

CIVILIAN is committed to ethical data handling practices including:

  • Transparency in information processing
  • Respect for privacy and cultural sensitivity
  • Evidence-based analysis and counter-messaging
  • Human oversight of automated processes
